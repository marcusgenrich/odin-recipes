Markey Scramble

Description

The highly praised, winner takes all type of free for all breakfast/lunch/dinner meal that (no)everyone's talking about!

It's hot hot hot!

Ingredients

  • Frozen tater tots
  • Eggses
  • Cheeses
  • Onions
  • Salsa
  • Ranch
  • Cholula/Ortega Taco sauce (both if you are awesome!)
  • Medium Giardineria

Steps

  1. Throw down onto a frying pan the onions, cheeses, and giardinerias to sautee. Meanwhile, put the tater tots in the air fryer for 9 minutes.
  2. Season and sauce the sautee while you wait for the tots.
  3. At 4-5 minutes remaining on the tots, crack 2 eggs onto the sautee, stir, and cook on 300 degree heat until about a minute before the tots are done.
  4. With 1 minute left, turn the pan off, take the tots out when they're done, put salsa, ranch, and pepper on them.
  5. Remove egg scramble and slop that down right on top of the tots.
  6. Yum!!!
