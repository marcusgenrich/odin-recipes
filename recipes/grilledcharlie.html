Grilled Charlie

Description

This famous dish is best known in the town/state/idk of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

In this midwestern twist, down under, you will see that objects don't always appear closer in the mirror.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • Pepperjack or habenero cheese slices
  • Salami
  • 1 Egg
  • Pepper (lots of pepper!)
  • Peri Peri mayonaisse (or chipotle ranch works too)
  • Butter
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Start by sauteeing the onions in a frying pan of your choice. While the onions sautee, stir occasionally, season frequently, smell constantly, you should be buttering one side of both slices of bread.
  2. Toss your salami in individual pieces onto the pan. Turn frequently to prevent burning.
  3. After a few minutes, crack the egg onto the pan and cook it your way. When the egg is nearing desired consistency, slap a piece of bread down and throw your cheese on that.
  4. Start moving all of your hot ingredients onto the future sandwich. Squirt some Peri Peri on the top before you seal it with the second piece of bread.
  5. Cook like a grilled cheese sandwich.
  6. Enjoy it and make sure you have a few napkins to hand!
