Pizza Rolls & Cheese Curds

Description

On one side you have cheese curds.

On the other, you have pizza rolls.

Ingredients

  • Frozen cheese curds
  • Pizza rolls
  • Ranch
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Take the stuff out of the freezer.
  2. Layer it on the bottom of the air fryer.
  3. Set air fryer for 10 minutes on french fry setting. Start it.
  4. Turn on the stovetop hood to prevent the smoke alarm from going off (it's a 50-50 it's still going to got off regardless).
  5. When timer is up, throw them in a bowl, grab a fork, sauce/spice it up, and enjoy!
